Biography

Hanh Hoang Le is a dedicated acupuncturist based in Sydney, with over a decade of clinical experience in traditional Chinese medicine. After earning her Bachelor of Health Science in Traditional Chinese Medicine from the University of Technology Sydney (UTS), Mei deepened her practice through intensive study in China and Japan, where she trained alongside respected practitioners in both urban hospitals and rural clinics. Her integrative approach combines classical acupuncture techniques with modern evidence-based care, offering tailored treatments that focus on holistic wellness. Today, she runs a thriving clinic in Sydney, helping patients restore balance, manage pain, and improve overall vitality.

Credentials

  • Certificate in Chinese Acupuncture Chengdou
